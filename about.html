Claire Shin

Hi, my name is Claire! I'm currently working in Mirae Asset's market making division, where I implement hedging strategies for an ETF market making system.

Prior to doing quant work, I did research in anomaly detection and medical imaging at Harvard and Seoul National University, building models for distribution shift detections, ultrasound tumor classification, and statistical evaluation for spatial computing. I also briefly worked on procedural generation systems and synthetic data tools for robotic perception, working with Blender, Python, and geometry/shader nodes for Infinigen at Princeton.

This notebook is where I track my interests over time.
